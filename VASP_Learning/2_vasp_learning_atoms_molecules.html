    本部分介绍了一些INCAR的变量

    1. 处理费米面的：ISMEAR & SIGMA
    2. 考虑电子自旋影响的：ISPIN
    3. 控制最大离子步的：NSW
    4. VASP算法选择：IBRION
    5. 自由度、海赛矩阵相关：NFREE
    6. 迭代步长：POTIM
    7. 局域化映射后处理：LORBIT
    8. 精确度相关设置：PREC
    9. 截断能量相关：ENMAX & ENCUT

    # 1. ISMEAR & SIGMA

    ISMEAR = 0   #Default: ISMEAR = 1 
SIGMA = 0.01 #Default: SIGMA = 0.2
    1
    2

    ISMEAR是对于费米面附近能量分布的修正，因为最基本的基于泡利不相容原理推到出的费米面和能量分布仅在温度取零时成立。而一般不会处理温度取零问题，所以需要对于费米面的附近能量分布进行修正。

    ISMEAR的取值:修正的算法的选取。

    SIGMA的取值：修正区域的宽窄，与温度有关的一个物理量，其值的选取对于计算结果的精度有影响。

    # 2. ISPIN

    ISPIN =  2  #Default: ISPIN = 1
    1

    ISPIN决定了是否考虑spin polarization的影响：

    ·ISPIN=1: non-spin-polarized calculations are performed.

    ·ISPIN=2: spin-polarized calculations (collinear) are performed.

    # 3. NSW

    NSW = 5    #Default: NSW = 0
    1

    NSW决定了the maximum number of ionic steps

    这里区分“离子步”和“电子步”：

    ·在确定离子实位置后，通过电子步，更新电荷密度，达到能量最低的位置，每更新一步，就是走了一个电子步；

    ·在走完一系列电子步后，更新计算离子实的位置，达到能量最低位置，每更新一步，就是走了一个离子步；

    # 5. NFREE

    NFREE = 2
#Default: NFREE	= 1	for IBRION = 2
#Default: NFREE	= 0	    else
    1
    2
    3

    定义：depending on IBRION, NFREE specifies the number of remembered steps in the history of ionic convergence runs, or the number of ionic displacements in frozen phonon calculations.

    总结：对于IBRION相关算法进行参数指定。如果针对复杂问题：NFREE can usually be set to a rather large value (i.e. 10-20)。如果针对简单问题，设置NFREE与问题自由度相同即可。

    注意：If NFREE is set to too large, the RMM-DIIS algorithm might diverge.

    POTIM = 0.02 # 0.02 A stepwidth 
#Default: POTIM	= none,	must be set if IBRION= 0 (MD)

#Default: POTIM = 0.5 if IBRION= 1, 2, and 3 (ionic relaxation), and 5 (up to VASP.4.6)

#Default: POTIM = 0.015 if IBRION=5, and 6 (as of VASP.5.1)
    1
    2
    3
    4
    5
    6

    定义：POTIM sets the time step in molecular dynamics or the step width in ionic relaxations

    说明：不同IBRION算法中，POTIM的含义不同

    1. 分子动力学中表示时间步长：For IBRION = 0, POTIM gives the time step (in fs) in all ab-initio Molecular Dynamics runs, it has to be supplied therefore, otherwise VASP crashes immediately after having started.

    2. 在这三个算法中表示修正步长：For IBRION =1, 2, and 3, which corresponds to ionic relaxation using a quasi-Newton algorithm, conjugate-gradient algorithm, and damped molecular dynamics, respectively, the POTIM tag serves as a scaling constant for the step widths. The quasi-Newton algorithm is especially sensitive to the choice of this parameter.

    3. 在这两个算法中表示离子移动步长：For IBRION = 5, and 6, a phonon calculations using the finite differences approach is done, where POTIM is the width of the displacement of each ion to calculate the Hessian Matrix.

    说明：在CO_Vibration案例中接触这一参数，即在IBRION = 5算法下，规定离子移动步长为0.02A。

    1

    定义：Selects a projection method onto local quantum numbers (lm) and writes PROCAR/PROOUT file.

    总结：一种定性的数值后处理方法，从KS轨道投影到局域化轨道进行性质分析，更偏向于物理、化学领域。笔者主要需要的物理量是离子实的位置、受力、体系能量。

    # 8. PREC

    PREC = Normal | Single | SingleN | Accurate | Low | Medium | High 
#PREC = Medium for VASP.4.X
#PREC = Normal since VASP.5.X
    1
    2
    3

    定义：PREC specifies the "precision" mode

    推荐：using PREC=Normal or PREC=Accurate.

    1. PREC=Normal can be used for most routine calculations

    2. PREC=Accurate increases the memory requirements somewhat, but it should be used (in combination with an increased value for ENCUT) when a very good accuracy is required, e.g., for accurate forces, for phonons and stress tensor or in general when second derivatives are computed

    总结：主要用Accurate，粗略估算时用Normal

    # 10. EDIFFG & EDIFF

    EDIFFG = [real]
#Default: EDIFFG = EDIFF×10 

EDIFF = [real]
#Default: EDIFF = e−4
    1
    2
    3
    4
    5

    EDIFF定义：EDIFF specifies the global break condition for the electronic SC-loop. EDIFF is specified in units of eV.

    EDIFFG定义：EDIFFG defines the break condition for the ionic relaxation loop.

    EDIFF推荐：

    1. for well converged calculations, decrease EDIFF to 1E-6

    2. for finite difference calculations, even EDIFF = 1E-7 might be required in order to obtain precise results

    3. for large systems with many atoms and/or when using meta-GGA functionals, attaining an energy convergence of 1E-8 or even 1E-7 might be difficult. So, overall EDIFF= 1E-6 is likely the best compromise

    EDIFFG说明：

    1. When EDIFFG is positive, the relaxation is stopped when the change of the total energy is smaller than EDIFFG between two ionic steps.

    2. When EDIFFG is negative, the relaxation is stopped when the norms of all the forces are smaller than |EDIFFG|. This is usually a more convenient setting.

    总结：EDIFF定义电子步收敛、EDIFFG定义离子步收敛，正EDIFFG表示按能量最小收敛、负EDIFFG表示按力平衡收敛。

    # 12. SMASS

    SMASS = -3       # Nose Hoover thermostat
#Default: SMASS = -3
    1
    2

    定义：SMASS controls the velocities during an ab-initio molecular-dynamics run

    不同SMASS控制的定义：

    1. For SMASS=-3 a microcanonical ensemble (NVE ensemble) is simulated (constant energy molecular dynamics). The calculated Hellmann-Feynman forces serve as an acceleration acting onto the ions. The total free energy (i.e. free electronic energy + Madelung energy of ions + kinetic energy of ions) is conserved.

    2. For SMASS=-2 the initial velocities are kept constant. This allows to calculate the energy for a set of different linear dependent positions (for instance frozen phonons, or dimers with varying bond lengths).Mind: if SMASS=-2 the actual steps taken are POTIM×(velocities-read-from-the-POSCAR-file). To avoid ambiguities, set POTIM=1.

    3. For SMASS=-1, in this case the velocities are scaled each NBLOCK step (starting at the first step i.e. MOD(NSTEP,NBLOCK)=1) to the temperature: T=TEBEG+(TEEND-TEBEG)×NSTEP/NSW,where NSTEP is the current step (starting from 1). This allows a continuous increase or decrease of the kinetic energy. In the intermediate period, a micro-canonical ensemble is simulated.

    4. For SMASS≥0, a canonical ensemble is simulated using the algorithm of Nosé. The Nosé mass controls the frequency of the temperature oscillations during the simulation.For SMASS=0, a Nosé-mass corresponding to period of 40 time steps will be chosen. The Nosé-mass should be set such that the induced temperature fluctuation show approximately the same frequencies as the typical 'phonon'-frequencies for the specific system. For liquids something like 'phonon'-frequencies might be obtained from the spectrum of the velocity auto-correlation function. If the ionic frequencies differ by an order of magnitude from the frequencies of the induced temperature fluctuations, Nosé thermostat and ionic movement might decouple leading to a non-canonical ensemble. The frequency of the approximate temperature fluctuations induced by the Nosé-thermostat is written to the OUTCAR file.

    总结：用VASP进行MD模拟时，更新速度的方法选择变量。

    1

    TEBEG定义：TEBEG sets the starting temperature (in K) for an ab-initio molecular dynamics run (IBRION=0) and other routines

    TEEND定义：TEEND sets the final temperature (in K) for an ab-initio molecular-dynamics run.
